Why ShopBuddy

Up until now shopping has been about browsing paper adverts and price checking in every store's app. ShopBuddy is a shopping utility that allows you to cut to the chase and find the cheapest place to buy your shopping list. Using Crowdsourced data, from loyal users like you, ShopBuddy always has the most up to date pricing information within the stores.

How ShopBuddy Works

Members are able to view saved shopping lists, create new ones and share shopping lists with others. After everything is added to the list, ShopBuddy will check each and every item at stores near you and determine the best possible shopping location to save you time and money.

ShopBuddy Premium
